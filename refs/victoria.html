Backstory: Being one of the youngest Celestial children, Victoria was kidnapped at an early age. The pirates had hoped to sell her off for a high price since she's a goddess. Unfortunately, their ship crash landed on Arcacia. Victoria was then adopted by the royal family and became the ruler of Arcacia for a short period of time. She was caught into it's political affairs before being assasinated by a woman who wanted her crown. During this time, Victoria returned to being a deity, and watched over Arcacia. Only one man was able to see and communicate with her, and helped her overthrow the Queen. Afterwrads, Arcacians built a shrine for Victoria and praised her as their deity, and gave her offerings as to please her so she would keep the nearby volcanoe dormant.
Over time, Celest was able to find Victoria and relocate her to the galaxy as the goddess of the stars.

Personality: Tsundere. Hot-headed with a soft-side. Victoria is pretty handy and likes to fix things herself. A workholic and believes that if you want something done right you have to do it yourself. Very independent. Maybe almost too independent.
